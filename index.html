About Me

I'm a native New Englander adjusting to Southern living in North Carolina. Though originally from the Boston area, I moved to NC after spending more than a decade in New York City. Boston Red Sox or Yankees? No comment.

The slower pace of Chapel Hill has its perks and there is good food to be had in the area. I love the beach but although it's relatively close, I don't make it to the coast as often as I would like. Actually, I'm pretty busy and don't always get to enjoy my hobbies. When I do have free time I love to exercise, especially swimming and spinning, reading, and learning new things. These days I'm focusing on learning how to code and how to speak German. I also love to eat, especially pizza, beer, and vegan chocolate cheesecake. I am not vegan or even vegetarian, but this particular vegan chocolate cheesecake recipe is awesome!
